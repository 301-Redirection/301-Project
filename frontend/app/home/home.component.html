BOT CONFIGURATOR


Customize your own bots to play how you want them to play

Create DOTA 2 Bots Your Way


Create and download custom bot scripts easily and quickly

The days of coding scripts tediously to customize bot behaviour are now in the past. This system aims to allow anyone to create their own scripts through the use of a graphical interface, while still allowing considerable customizability in areas such as Team Desires, Heroes, Abilities, and Items.

CREATE



Create and Edit custom bot scripts

Give your custom scripts a title and description, then begin creating your custom bot script. Create custom team desires to your bots to follow based on events that occur during the game. Pick the heroes and the pools that they should belong to, then customize each hero's abilities and item builds, all using a simple, powerful interface. Once you are satisfied with the bot script you have created simply save it, to be able to pick up later.

GENERATE



Download the custom bot script you have created

Once you have created a custom bot script, simply click generate and our system will create your custom script by generating the Lua scripts needed to implement the custom behaviour you requested. Once the scripts are created they are downloaded onto your system, after which you just need to follow our simple installation guide to install the script to Dota 2.

PLAY



Play. Create endless custom bot behaviours and strategies

Begin to play against your custom bot script after following the easy installation guide. You can use the custom bot script you have created to improve your Dota skills against different strategies without any other influences affecting your game play. Improve your Dota skills, quickly, simply and easily.
